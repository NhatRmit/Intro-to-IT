Project Ideas

Overview


The project called "Han and the way back home". This project is an Indie game using less and simple tools that only one person is able to make it without teams to support. It is not going to be hard in the gameplay, besides, it is going to focus on the content, graphics and also the sound to attract those who want to enjoy game as the movie more than how it played. The project is inspired by the game called "Ori and the Blind Forest". However, that game is good at all such as gameplay, plot, music and also graphics. The game is about an adventure of Han finding way back home. It is actually about the dog, Han is his name.

Motivation


I believe that my game will be played and responsed by many people because it is about the smart of how dog can find their way back to home whenever incidentally lost by their boss. It is not about fighting and not having violent scenes that could make someone hate it because of the way dogs being treated. It is going to have tactic and using brain to pass obstacles in game more than killing it or super power to pass it. Lastly, the dog character is not only one, you will have many choices of many popular types of dogs.

Description


There are some features of this game are able to attract fans of Indie games. One of those is the content, which is one of the kind. Because as far as I have known, there is no such a game talking about pets. "Han and the way back home" will show you all the obstacles that an ordinary dog can suffer whenever it is lost their boss or lost the way coming home. Particularly, the game will show players all emotions and feelings showing on the dog's face how they feel exact like in the reality. Especially, dogs have feelings and characteristics like human, thus, the game is supposed to develop Han's feelings and emotions during the games, from "a little boy" of his boss, to an actual "man". Then the game is going to require players to use their brains more than their hands on keyboard. Cause all the hurdles are about trick questions and you need to pass it smartly. Depending on how you pass the first trial, the second will be more difficult than the previous. For instance, being caught by the group of people who want to sell dogs for butchers, being kidnapped by those who hate dogs and want to punish them. I want to remind that there will not be blood, the dog is just being caught and it will find the way out. However, time will be limited and when it is over, game will be stopped. Guaranteely, there will not have blood. Time limitation combine with the rushing music will be the best feature of this game. Due to the first Indie game having time limitation. Most of the game only has timing to pass the hurdles like "Limbo" or "Inside". Eventhough the game is about a dog and his name is Han, the player is able to create their own dogs and also cats. Then they can experience the game in the hardest mode like exactly in reality and all the situations will not be the same orders. Specially, gameplay is not complicated, there are four movements, jump, walking slowly, lying, and hold the breath controls, game is not using the mouse to play. Sounds and audios will not let players disappointed by making players get through their feelings from nervous to excited and from peaceful to hateful, angry and then sympathy. Furthermore, eventhough it is only an Indie game, it is made very carefully with all the logical reality. As like when the player holds the breath control long time, heartbeat will be stronger than usual, HP bar will be minus, the heartbeat can be easy to recognize because the project makes it red full the screen. Or if the player pass the food or drinks but do not consume it, Han will be hungry and thirsty, as a result, HP bar will be down until it is eaten something. The stamina is also be impacted if Han keeps running all the time. This feature makes the players calculate more on how to play Han logically. Every game has different features to attract the players. Hopefully, my the description can reach out to all your hearts and curiosity to enjoy this game. Lastly, it is a free to play game.
